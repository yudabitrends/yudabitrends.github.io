**SpecRNA-QA** applies spectral graph theory to RNA 3D structure quality assessment. By computing features from the graph Laplacian spectrum of RNA contact networks, we achieve reference-free quality scoring that correlates well with established metrics like RMSD and GDT-TS.

Key features:
- Model-free spectral features from RNA contact graphs
- Reference-free quality assessment pipeline
- Benchmarked on RNA-Puzzles and CASP-RNA datasets
