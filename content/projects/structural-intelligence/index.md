The **Structural Intelligence (SI)** framework is a unifying theoretical foundation for cross-disciplinary research. It reconceptualizes core concepts like redundancy, spectral structure, and timescale separation across neuroscience, financial systems, and artificial intelligence.

Research threads include:
- **Redundancy as structure**: Optimal information redundancy for learning and generalization
- **Spectral coherence**: Model-free metrics for quality assessment of complex structures
- **Timescale physics**: How hidden forcing and timescale coalescence shape observable dynamics
- **Financial connectomics**: Brain-inspired frameworks for modeling latent market dynamics
