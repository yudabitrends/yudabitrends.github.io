abstract: "We introduce the Spectral Coherence Index (SCI), a rotation-invariant metric derived from the participation-ratio effective rank of inter-model pairwise distance-variance matrices. Tested on a cohort of 110 NMR ensembles, SCI distinguished experimental protein structures from synthetic noise-based controls with an AUC-ROC of 0.973, achieving 95.5% sensitivity and 89.1% specificity."
